Food Ninja

This is a cooking App design inspired my mom who can always make wonders by useing foof ingredients left in the refriferator .

Introduction

This App called “FoodNinja” which offers recipes for a healthy and quick meal which can be easily processed by novices. It is also dedicated to dealing with the food ingredients left in the refrigerator in an effective way which don’t need to be worried about, just like what a Ninja can do. Users can enter the quantity of ingredients or just scan the label to input the information and then get the highly matched recipe. In addition, the app can generate meal plan according to the users’ health data, existing ingredients and taste preferences. Users can also update their own secret recipes to the system and contribute to the cooking community.

I start this App because many people find it difficult to cook and plan for a healthy meal. It is also frequent to see my families struggling with the ingredients left in the refrigerator. From my research result, many people are facing these kind of problems. There is a tendency of people who live alone to cook more at home instead of dining out, so I think this App can help those workers to have a healthy meal or deal with their ingredients after their long day working. Many old people, according to my research, also have problem of affording a healthy recipe. My function of providing a list of recipes with shopping list can definitely help with calculating the budget.

Framework

“FoodNinja” is special in the way that it is more than a cookbook because it has the function of matching a recipe by knowing what ingredients do users have. Users in this way have the mobility of cooking a healthy meal by using what they have at home. I dedicate to creating a user-friendly App which is easy to use because this App is open to all ages and especially for those busy people who love cooking. I have icons of different ingredients which is easier for users to select. After selecting the right one, people can enter the quantity by clicking on the button below. Then the system can input the data to “storage” and come up with a recipe that covers the ingredients that one has. I also implement the QR scanning system to enter the ingredients to the system as an option. Though it may not be applicable at this moment, I design this function because I believe this App will meet the future technological advancement which enables this usage and brings much convenience to this cooking App. Other than this special function, people can also just search for a recipe and also get recommendation for popular recipes at the home page, and get the shopping list in the popular recipe’s page by clicking the icon of “shopping cart”(users can also delete the ingredients they already have in a previous page), thus making this App a multifunctional one. The function of uploading one’s own recipe makes users interact with this App more.

Interaction Design

“FoodNinja” aims to be a good assistant in the kitchen; a responsible manager of one’s eating plans; as well as a platform for users to see other people’s recipes as well as share their own specialty dishes!
